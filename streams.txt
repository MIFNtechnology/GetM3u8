## FORMAT: <channel name> || <channel id> || <category> || <channel logo>
## <channel name> can be anything - either stream or channel name. It will appear on the EPG though.
## <channel id> is used to match EPG data to the M3U8 playlist, so make it short and with no spaces. End it in '.yt' to show its from YouTube.
##<category> is used to group streams, so something like 'Music' or 'News' is recommended.
##<channel logo> use https url for view image logo.
##
## Examples:
## Twitch:
## CDawgVA || CDVA.tt || Gaming || https://../logo.jpg
## https://www.twitch.tv/cdawgva
## Dailymotion:
## C-News || CNews.dm || News || https://../logo.jpg
## https://www.dailymotion.com/video/x3b68jn
## YouTube:
## Sky News || SkyNews.yt || News || https://../logo.jpg
